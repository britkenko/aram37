The Unseen Danger on Our Roads

Despite advances in vehicle technology, the risk to pedestrians, cyclists, and other vulnerable road users (VRUs) remains critically high. Standard vehicle sensors have a fundamental limitation: they can't see around corners or through obstructions.

A Multi-Layered Technology Stack

COMET fuses multiple communication protocols, creating a robust and reliable system that is greater than the sum of its parts. This data fusion provides unparalleled contextual awareness.

Data Fusion Process

  • 1. Detect Presence (BLE): A vehicle gets a general warning: "VRU nearby".
  • 2. Pinpoint Location (UWB): Provides centimeter-level accuracy.
  • 3. Add Context (GPS): Places the VRU on a map.
  • 4. Predict & Act (AI): Fuses data to predict a potential collision and act.
