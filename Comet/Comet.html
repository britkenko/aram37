Why Conventional Sensors Fail

Autonomous and ADAS-equipped vehicles rely on Line-of-Sight sensors like cameras, LIDAR, and radar. They are blind to hidden dangers, creating unavoidable collisions in common urban scenarios.

A Multi-Layered Technology Stack

COMET fuses multiple communication protocols, creating a robust and reliable system that is greater than the sum of its parts. This data fusion provides unparalleled contextual awareness.

Communication Protocol Roles

Each technology provides a unique layer of information, from general presence to precise location, which COMET's AI fuses into a single, actionable insight for the vehicle.

How Data Fusion Creates Safety

1

Detect Presence (BLE)

A vehicle first gets a general warning: "VRU nearby" from a low-power Bluetooth signal.

2

Pinpoint Location (UWB)

Ultra-Wideband provides centimeter-level accuracy, distinguishing if the person is on the sidewalk or street.

3

Add Context (GPS)

GPS data places the VRU on a map, correlating their position with a crosswalk or school zone.

4

Predict & Act (AI)

The vehicle's AI fuses this data to predict a potential collision and preemptively slows down, seconds before visual confirmation.
