A Unified Network for a Smarter, Safer Green City

This interactive application explores a patented 6G-enabled modular network that transforms urban infrastructure. The system uses a swarm of micro-drones, managed by a central AI, to perform dual roles: providing helpful civilian services by default, and instantly converting into a coordinated defense system when a threat is detected.

Dual-Mode Operations

The network's core innovation is its ability to seamlessly switch between two operational modes. Use the toggle below to see how the system's objectives, drone payloads, and AI priorities adapt in real-time.

Civilian Support Mode

In its default state, the network focuses on improving urban life. Drones are equipped with specialized tools to perform helpful tasks, coordinated by the AI to maximize efficiency and safety.

The Technology Core

📡

6G: The Neural Network

6G communication is the backbone of the system, offering a quantum leap in performance over 5G for real-time, ultra-reliable coordination.

🧠

AI: The Central Brain

A central AI core analyzes data, optimizes drone routes, manages missions, and detects threats to switch the network to defense mode.

🚁

Drones: The Active Multi Agents

Lightweight, modular drones act as the system's hands and eyes. Their payloads can be automatically swapped at hubs to adapt to missions.
