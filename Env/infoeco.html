The 4-Phase Restoration Cycle: From Data to Forest

This invention creates a closed-loop, adaptive system. It's a continuous cycle of planning, deploying, monitoring, and learning, making restoration a data-driven science.

1

Survey & Plan

UAVs with LiDAR create a 3D map. AI identifies the perfect micro-sites for planting.

2

Deploy

A UAV swarm executes the plan, deploying thousands of BRPs per hour with precision.

3

Monitor

UAVs act as "data mules," flying over the site to wirelessly collect data from BRP sensors.

4

Analyze & Adapt

AI analyzes the data, verifies success, and informs future missions for continuous improvement.
