Conclusion: Architecture Matters

Beyond the staggering cost difference, the models' qualitative performance reveals a fundamental divergence in their ability to learn and adapt. This shows that AI is here to assist in any field, not replace jobs.

Deep Learning: Brittle Specialization

The DL model performs well on the single task it was last trained on but suffers from catastrophic forgetting. When fine-tuned for a new job, its expert knowledge of the previous job is lost. It is extremely rigid and cannot dynamically synthesize knowledge.

Panacea Cortex: Resilient Partnership

The PaCo model also performs at a high level, but its understanding is deeper. Crucially, it retains all knowledge. The Adaptive Core integrates new information without overwriting old, allowing PaCo to seamlessly switch between its expert roles and even combine them in novel ways.
