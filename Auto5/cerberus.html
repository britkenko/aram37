CERBEROS

Decentralized Pan Network Auto Driving System

Revolutionary autonomous driving architecture featuring decentralized AI negotiation, networked fail-safe coordination, and ethical decision-making for the future of transportation

Core System Modules

🧠

PaCoM - Pan-network Consensus Module

Decentralized AI negotiation system enabling multiple autonomous vehicles to collaboratively negotiate driving decisions without central control. Implements consensus algorithms for real-time cooperation.

Peer-to-Peer Negotiation
Direct vehicle communication for maneuver coordination
Consensus Algorithms
Distributed decision-making without centralized control
Real-time Optimization
Dynamic traffic flow optimization through cooperation
Conflict Resolution
Automated resolution of driving conflicts and disputes
🛡️

NUA - Networked Unified Autonomy

Advanced fail-safe coordination system that enables multiple vehicles and infrastructure to collaboratively respond to emergencies and system failures, ensuring network-wide safety.

Failure Detection
Real-time monitoring of vehicle system health
Collective Override
Network intervention for malfunctioning vehicles
Emergency Coordination
Synchronized emergency responses across multiple vehicles
Redundant Safety
Multi-layered backup systems through network cooperation
⚖️

SOMA - Safety-Oriented Moral Arbiter

Ethical decision-making module that remains dormant during normal operation and activates only when unavoidable collision scenarios require moral judgment and ethical choices.

Ethical Framework
Pre-programmed moral guidelines for decision-making
Harm Minimization
Algorithms to minimize overall harm in unavoidable scenarios
Transparent Decisions
Logged decision processes for accountability and review
Dormant Mode
Inactive during normal operation, only triggers in extremes
👁️

Multi-Sensor Fusion System

Advanced perception system combining infrared, LiDAR, and visual sensors for robust environmental understanding under all conditions, with dynamic weighting optimization.

Infrared Detection
Heat signature detection for living beings and objects
LiDAR Precision
3D mapping and precise distance measurements
Visual Recognition
Object classification and traffic sign reading
Dynamic Weighting
Adaptive sensor prioritization based on conditions
