The Cerberus Architecture

Auto5 Cerberus's revolutionary architecture consists of five integrated intelligence layers, each providing unparalleled autonomous driving capabilities.

Perception Layer: Multi-Modal Sensor Fusion Engine

The foundation layer that provides comprehensive environmental awareness through advanced sensor fusion, processing data from LiDAR, radar, cameras, and ultrasonic sensors in real-time.

Cognition Layer: Predictive Reasoning Engine

The intelligence layer that provides predictive reasoning capabilities, anticipating the behavior of other road users and making decisions based on comprehensive situational awareness.

Control Layer: Precision Actuation System

The execution layer that translates cognitive decisions into precise vehicle control commands, ensuring smooth, safe, and efficient driving maneuvers.

Guardian Layer: Safety & Redundancy Framework

The safety layer that provides multiple redundancy systems and fail-safe mechanisms, ensuring the vehicle can always maintain safe operation even under extreme conditions.

Learning Layer: Continuous Improvement Engine

The adaptation layer that continuously learns from every driving scenario, improving performance and adapting to new environments and driving conditions over time.
